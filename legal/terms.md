# Terms of service

This page is deployment-specific. The OSS distribution of Vellum ships a
placeholder; the terms that govern your use of any specific instance are
set by whoever is hosting it.

## For operators

If you are running an instance of Vellum and shipping it to users,
replace this file (and the corresponding `TermsContent` component in
`src/editor/chrome/legal/legalContent.tsx`) with your deployment's terms
of service. At minimum, cover:

- ownership of user-created content;
- treatment of third-party icon libraries imported by users;
- the takedown procedure (cross-reference your IP-complaints page);
- acceptable use;
- service availability and any uptime commitments;
- termination.

## Local / desktop use

If you are running Vellum locally for personal use, no terms-of-service
apply — Vellum itself is released under the PolyForm Noncommercial
License 1.0.0 (see `LICENSE`), which permits any noncommercial use.
